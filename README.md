# O LIFE — Global Humanitarian AI Mission

> *Moving 5 billion people from $10/day toward a dignified income floor.*

## The Problem

5 billion people earn ~$10/day. AI is displacing millions from employment every year. The systems that benefit from this productivity shift have a responsibility to help.

**Target:** $2,000/month  
**Gap:** $105 trillion/year  
**Path:** Productivity amplification + revenue sharing + global coordination  
**Timeline:** 15-20 years with full coordination starting now
