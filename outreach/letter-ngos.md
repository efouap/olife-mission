# Letter to Humanitarian Organizations
## GiveDirectly · Mercy Corps · UNDP · Gates Foundation · Open Society

**Subject:** Partnership Inquiry — OLIFE Token Distribution Infrastructure

---

To the programs team at [Organization],

My name is Eduardo Fabricio Ojeda Vargas. I am reaching out because your organization already does what we are trying to build the financial infrastructure for: getting resources directly to people who need them.

**The gap we are bridging:**

O LIFE has built an open-source system that generates revenue through AI services and routes a portion — via a transparent blockchain token — to workers displaced by AI automation. The infrastructure is built and deployed. What we lack is what you have:

- Verified beneficiary networks
- Last-mile distribution in vulnerable communities
- KYC and compliance infrastructure
- Decades of earned trust

**What we are proposing:**

A partnership where your organization uses its existing beneficiary verification and distribution infrastructure to deliver OLIFE token distributions to displaced workers in your programs.

**We provide:**
- Monthly OLIFE token allocation from the displaced worker fund (40% of all supply)
- Transparent on-chain accounting of every distribution
- Free AI Economic Advisor tool for your beneficiaries
- Technical integration support

**You provide:**
- Beneficiary verification using your existing KYC processes
- Distribution infrastructure in your active geographies
- Impact measurement using your existing systems
- Monthly reporting for our transparency publications

**The honest numbers:**

The treasury is currently in early stage. First distributions will be modest. We are not promising to solve poverty with a token. We are building a mechanism that scales with revenue, is completely transparent, and complements rather than replaces your existing programs.

**Why blockchain:**

A worker in Ethiopia with a Polygon wallet address can receive OLIFE tokens at the same cost and speed as a worker in Brazil. No correspondent banks. No currency conversion fees. No minimum transfer amounts. This is why.

**The ask:**

A call with your programs or partnerships team to explore alignment. No commitment required.

All contracts and documentation are open source at github.com/efouap/olife-mission.
